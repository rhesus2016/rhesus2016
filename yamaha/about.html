introduce yamaha

Yamaha Music Korea, established as a local subsidiary of Yamaha Japan, aims to contribute to the promotion of cultural exchange between the two countries and to the development of Korean music and music education.

Starting with the slogan of "Making Touching Together", we’ve developed a world-renowned ‘grand piano’ as well as musical instruments and ‘acoustics’ that produce and sell upright pianos, digital pianos, portable keyboards, synthesizers, guitars, drums, professional audio, It is a total equipment maker.
